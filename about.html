Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you use for web design?

We specialize in WordPress, Webflow, and custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript solutions, tailoring our approach to your specific needs.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions?

Yes, we provide full e-commerce website development using platforms like WooCommerce and Shopify.

What software do you use for 3D/2D animation?

We primarily use Blender for 3D animation and Adobe After Effects for 2D animation, ensuring high-quality results.

Can you create animated explainer videos?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating engaging explainer videos that effectively communicate your message.

How does AI enhance video and image production?

AI technologies allow for advanced editing, style transfer, and even content generation, significantly speeding up the production process.

Is AI-generated content copyright-free?

While AI-generated content is a grey area in copyright law, we ensure all our deliverables are original and tailored to your needs.

What file formats do you provide for logo designs?

We provide logos in various formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, and JPG to ensure compatibility across all platforms.

Do you offer business card printing services?

Yes, we offer full business card design and printing services, using high-quality materials and finishes.

Can you edit videos shot on smartphones?

Absolutely! We can enhance and edit videos from any source, including smartphones, to create professional-looking content.

Do you offer color grading services?

Yes, we provide advanced color grading services to give your videos and images a polished, professional look.
